Sweet Potato Waffles

⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ☆

Prep Time: 30 Min | Cook Time: 30 Min | Yield: 6 waffles

Savory waffles made with Sweet potato with a hint of Ginger

Ingredients

  • 2 separated eggs
  • 1/4 C Oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 3/4 C milk
  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 tsp Shredded Ginger
  • 3/4 C Mashed Sweet Potatoes
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Shallots
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Chives
  • 1/4 C Cornmeal
  • 1 C Flour

Instructions

  1. Add the egg yolks, oil, salt, cayenne, sugar, ginger, shallots, sweet potatoes (steam and mash before), and milk and mix well.
  2. Next add the cornmeal, chives, and flour and baking powder
  3. Whip the egg whites until stiff and fold in
  4. Cook until golden brown in a waffle iron. Serve with butter and Wilted Greens or Honey.
