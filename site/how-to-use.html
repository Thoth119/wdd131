How to Use the MTG Curve Calculator

Home How to Use Easy Grading

Follow these steps to analyze your Magic: The Gathering deck:

  1. Select Spell or Land from the dropdown.
  2. Click mana buttons to add mana symbols (up to 10, max 15 total cost), (the number button requires field entry).
  3. For lands, check "Enters Tapped" if applicable(not implemented).
  4. Enter the amount to add and click Add to Deck.
  5. View your deck list, calculate card playability and clear to restart.
